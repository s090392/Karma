Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026 · Version 1.0
The short version: KARMA does not know who you are. Your employer, salary, and task descriptions never leave your device. The only thing on our server is your email address and three score numbers. If you delete your account, we delete those within 24 hours.

What we store on our server

📧 Email address — used only to send your login link and identify your account if you return. Never shared. Never used for marketing.
🔢 Score numbers — risk score, safety score, logic quotient. Three numbers with no context that cannot identify you.
💳 Subscription status — your plan name and billing period. Payment details are held by Razorpay only — we never see your card or bank details.
📅 Created date and country — the date you joined and the country you selected.

What stays on your device only

Your employer name, salary range, task atoms, career history, identity answers, city, gender, age range, and mission progress are stored only in your browser's local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers. We cannot see it, access it, or delete it. Only you can — by clearing your browser storage.
⚠ Important: If you clear your browser data, use a different browser, or switch devices — your assessment data will be lost. Your score history is backed up on our server. Take a screenshot of your profile before clearing browser data.

Your rights

Delete — Delete your account from your profile page. We delete your email and scores from our server within 24 hours.
Export — Download all your data (device + scores) as a JSON file from your profile page.
Access — Email privacy@biddano.com to request a copy of what we hold about you.
Questions — Contact our Grievance Officer (DPDP Act 2023) at privacy@biddano.com. We respond within 72 hours.

Cookies

We use one cookie — your anonymous session token. No advertising cookies. No tracking pixels. No analytics cookies. No consent banner needed.
